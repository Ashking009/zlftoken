About Us

ZillonLife is a young and vibrant e-commerce company with more than four years of experience in the market with an aim to offer premium products at affordable prices. We cater to a wide array of merchandise, including Health & Wellness, Lifestyle, Air Purifier, and Home Security buyers, Insurance, Financial Services, and International Education.

With the blockchain and cryptocurrency market establishing itself, ZillonLife is introducing the ZLF token as the primary token for its international product line–Online education­. Additionally, the existing business verticals, products, and services will also be using ZLF as its underlying token for transactions within the ZLF ecosystem.


The ZLF token will be the proof of ownership, and one will need to hold a minimum of 1000. Accordingly, 1% of the monthly turnover will be paid as dividends to ZLF HOLDERS, and 10% of annual profits will be paid as dividends to ZLF HOLDERS.

      With ZLF, you can earn passive income. ZLF holders receive dividends every month. 1% of Zillonlife monthly turnover is paid to dividends holders, and 10% of the annual profit will be paid to holders.

      You can hold ZLF tokens in the Tron wallet. Use the Tron wallet app / Tron link extension in the browser.
